Our Services

It has been observed in most people that they shy away from putting their views forward or formulating their own ideas. Over time it is affecting the ability of people to think at all! No people are born equal in this world or have the same capacity to think. But this is not the age to be intimadated by the smart ones. This is the age to test the level of smartness of your own brain.

Discussion


We believe that there is no better way than discussion to put forth an idea formulating in your brain. The human ability of speech is what distinguishes us from the other creatures. We decide to use it to encourage the human ability to think on the whole. When people are allowed to speak their heart out without a judge to give a verdict, they enhance their power to think.

Private Chat


In the middle of the discussion, you ever come across a person who matches your frequency like no one else does? Just start a private chat with the concerned person and continue your discussion on the desired topic with no hassle of entertaining others' views in between.

View Trending Topics


Do you feel like you are an introvert? Don't feel so keen on sharing your views yet are interested to know what's the latest trending topics people around you are discussing about? We give you an opportunity to do just that. View the most popular discussions and be a spectator till you feel comfortable and confident to put forth your point.

Find people with similar interests

You have an idea in mind, but find difficulty formulating it and putting it forth? There are others out there facing the same difficulty. We help to find people whose interests match with yours and who mutually help each other to tranform a thought to an idea.

About Us

Have an idea in mind? Why mull over it? Here's a quick and direct way to review the reaction of others on what you think. Ever feel alone? Ever feel like people around you don't get what you're trying to say, or are simply disinterested to listen? Then remember, you have a mind and a right to think, and there are others out there who are interested in what you think. Here's a forum to exercise your right and share your views with others. It is the perfect platform for the introvert ones where you can speak your heart out and not be judged. And for the knowledgeable ones, knowledge grows as you share it. And what better platform to share it than here, with people who are equally willing to gain knowledge.

Who We Are

Imagine what the human race would be without the creativity of it's brain! This gave us the idea of creating a forum that would urge people to use their brain for the sake of fun. No one is inferior or superior in this world, it's just that everyone is different. So here we present a forum where anyone can freely communicate and put forth their views on a subject of their choice.

What We Do

A human brain never stops working, it generates millions of thoughts in a single day. If these thoughts are given a forum to be captured rather than being wasted away, the world would be such a better place! And we do exactly that.

Why Choose Us

We aim at providing a quick post and immediate response forum for sharing the views of people who are interested to share, among people who are interested to listen and review upon it, on topics that genuinely interest them. Hang on to us if you wanna explore the potential of your brain you never thought you possessed!
